Locked Inside

Locked Inside is a 3D game that combines investigation, suspense, and stealth, developed to raise awareness about depression during the Covid-19 pandemic. As the lead programmer, I spearheaded the team at Pudimdim Games, implementing mechanics involving the exploration of a symbolic environment, solving puzzles, and evading an enemy entity representing the protagonist's depressive side. Utilizing Unity 2021.3, we crafted an immersive experience that not only entertains but also fosters empathy and understanding of mental health challenges during times of isolation and uncertainty. Here you can play the game.
